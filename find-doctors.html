How this directory works

We want to be upfront about where this data comes from.

Pulled from OpenStreetMap data

Listings come from OpenStreetMap, a free, community-maintained map used worldwide — not generated or guessed. Coverage is best in larger cities.

Always double-check before visiting

Hours, phone numbers, and availability can change. Call ahead to confirm before traveling to a clinic.

Works for any city

Search isn't limited to one location — type any city or town and we'll look for doctors and clinics there.

No bookings handled here

We connect you to the doctor's real contact details — you book or visit directly with the clinic, the same as calling them yourself.
