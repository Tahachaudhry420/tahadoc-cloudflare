What TahaDoc Covers

Health topics, sorted by how urgent they are.

From everyday concerns to situations that need a doctor immediately, here's how TahaDoc organizes the full range of symptoms people search for.

Routine

Everyday symptoms

The concerns most people deal with day to day.

  • Cold, flu & sore throat
  • Headaches & migraines
  • Allergies & hay fever
  • Skin irritation & minor rashes
  • Digestive discomfort & nausea
  • Minor aches, strains & sprains
  • Sleep difficulties
Priority

Worth seeing a doctor soon

Cases where a real consultation is the recommended next step.

  • Fever lasting more than 2 days
  • Chronic condition check-ins (asthma, diabetes, hypertension)
  • Medication questions & interactions
  • Recurring or worsening pain
  • Mental health & stress concerns
  • Women's health questions
  • Pediatric symptoms parents are unsure about
Urgent

See a doctor now

TahaDoc directs you straight to the doctor directory for these.

  • Severe, sudden, or worsening pain
  • Difficulty breathing
  • High fever that won't come down
  • Signs of infection spreading
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Symptoms after an injury or fall
  • Any symptom that feels like an emergency
