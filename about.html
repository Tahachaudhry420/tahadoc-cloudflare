About TahaDoc

Built to close the gap between worry and a real appointment.

Most people don't need a diagnosis at 2am — they need a clearer sense of what's happening and a fast way to reach someone who can actually help.

What we believe

Four principles guide how TahaDoc is built.

Real data over generated guesses

Our doctor directory uses OpenStreetMap listings, not AI-generated names or numbers — because made-up contact details could send someone to a clinic that doesn't exist.

When in doubt, point to a real doctor

The symptom checker is a preview feature. For anything beyond a routine question, TahaDoc's job is to get you to the doctor directory, not to guess on its own.

Your search is yours

Health questions are personal. What you search and ask is never sold or shared.

Plain language, always

No jargon left unexplained, no fine print hidden where you won't find it.

What TahaDoc is — and isn't

Being clear about limits is part of being trustworthy.

⚠ Not a diagnosis tool. The symptom checker is a product preview meant to show how the experience feels. It does not currently analyze real symptoms or produce a medical diagnosis.
⚠ Not for emergencies. If you may be having a medical emergency, contact local emergency services immediately rather than using this site.
⚠ Not a booking system. The doctor directory shows real contact details, but you call or visit the clinic directly — TahaDoc doesn't manage appointments.
⚠ Listings can change. Phone numbers, hours, and addresses come from public data and may be outdated. Always confirm with the clinic before visiting.
