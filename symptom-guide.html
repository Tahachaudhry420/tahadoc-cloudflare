What this tool does — and doesn't do

Being upfront about limits is part of being useful.

✅ Suggests a category of doctor — like "General Physician" or "Dermatologist" — based on simple keyword matching in what you type.
⚠ Does not diagnose conditions. It never tells you what illness you might have — only what kind of specialist typically handles that type of concern.
⚠ Not for emergencies. If something feels urgent or severe, skip this tool and call your local emergency number or go to the nearest doctor immediately.
⚠ Always defers to a real doctor. The actual answer — what's going on and what to do — comes from the doctor you visit, not from this page.
